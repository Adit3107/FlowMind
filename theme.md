# Flowbase Theme

## Mood
- Cozy, modern, and clean.
- Softer than a stark SaaS shell, but fresher and lighter than a muted beige workspace.
- Calm surfaces, crisp type, colorful Lucide accents, and generous breathing room.
- Keep the warmth, but avoid a dull or washed-out finish.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm cream with a brighter, fresher sunlit tint.
- Sidebar and panels: white and near-white surfaces for clarity.
- Text: deep slate for primary copy, mid-slate for supporting copy.
- Primary accent: coral-orange for the main action and brand mark.
- Secondary accents: sky, amber, violet, rose, emerald, and indigo for icons and highlights.
- Borders: pale beige-gray so panels stay airy.

## Typography
- Use a clean sans-serif UI face with slightly tight tracking for headings.
- Keep headings semibold and compact.
- Keep labels and navigation smaller than the main content so the dashboard feels dense but not crowded.

## Spacing And Radius
- Prefer 12-24px spacing inside panels, with wider spacing between major page regions.
- Use rounded-xl to rounded-[24px] surfaces depending on hierarchy.
- Keep the sidebar a little smaller and denser than the main workspace.

## Components
- Sidebar:
  - Collapsible on desktop.
  - Icon-only mode when collapsed.
  - Group labels should be compact uppercase labels.
  - Navigation rows should stay compact and smaller than the content panels.
  - Navigation icons should stay colorful and distinct.
  - Footer area should carry account/status utility content.
- Cards and panels:
  - Use soft shadows sparingly.
  - Avoid heavy outlines or dark fills unless the item is active.
  - Favor layered white surfaces with subtle tint variations.

## Behavior
- The first impression should feel productive, calm, and ready for work.
- The layout should support dashboard, AI, notes, tasks, whiteboard, and spaces without forcing a single narrow workflow.
- Freshness matters more than warmth alone, so keep contrast clean and color accents lively.
